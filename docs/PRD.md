# Budget Beacon — Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Identity
**Name:** Budget Beacon
**One-Line Description:** A private, local-first household budgeting cockpit that helps a couple or family see income, bills, debts, savings goals, and budget pressure clearly.

## Core Systems
1. **Pay Path:** The required-money system (bills, debts, minimums, timeline, required monthly outflow).
2. **Stash Map:** The savings system (goals, targets, monthly contributions, deadline health).

## Jobs-To-Be-Done
- Enter incomes and bills to see true leftover money.
- Add debts to see required debt payments pressure.
- Create savings goals to verify scheduled contributions.
- Warn clearly when the plan is unrealistic.
- Export and print monthly reports.
- Maintain full offline data ownership without bank sync.

## Target Users
Couples/families managing shared finances, needing visibility and manual control without cloud syncing.

## Non-Goals (MVP)
- No Plaid/Bank Sync.
- No automated credit pulls.
- No payment processing.
- No multi-device sync or backend infrastructure.
